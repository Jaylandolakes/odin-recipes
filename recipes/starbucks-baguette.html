Description

The Starbucks Ham & Swiss Baguette is a sandwich made with a crusty, rustic-style baguette filled with sliced ham and Swiss cheese. The bread has a firm and chewy texture, while the ham provides a savory, slightly salty flavor, balanced by the nutty and mild taste of the Swiss cheese. Overall, the sandwich has a classic, simple flavor profile, combining the richness of the cheese with the savory ham, complemented by the crispness of the baguette. Some versions may include mustard or butter for added tang and richness.


Ingredients

  • 1 fresh baguette (crusty style)

  • 4-6 slices of thinly sliced ham

  • 2-3 slices of Swiss cheese

  • 1-2 teaspoons Dijon mustard (optional)

  • Butter (optional, for spreading)


Steps

  1. Preheat your oven or toaster oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Slice the baguette in half lengthwise.

  3. Spread a thin layer of Dijon mustard or butter on one or both halves of the bread (optional).

  4. Layer the ham slices evenly on the bottom half of the baguette.

  5. Place the Swiss cheese slices on top of the ham.

  6. Close the baguette by placing the top half over the filling.

  7. Wrap the sandwich in foil and place it in the oven for 8-10 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy.

  8. Remove from the oven, unwrap, and serve warm.
